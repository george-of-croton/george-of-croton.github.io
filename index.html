Welcome to GDS, George's Development Space! I know, the name is pretty lame so consider a working title until I come up with something more snappy. Happy to to take suggestions!

What is GDS? by now you may be asking yourself this question. Don't worry I wonder that too sometimes. From the information I have gathered, by trawling the deep web, and analyzing thousands, if not hundreds of thousands of the most cutting edge memes, wins, fails, EPIC fails and lols, is that GDS is a space to present extremely amateur displays of web development and coding. You may mock the simplicity and pink color scheme now, but once GDS achieves it final form no one will be laughing.

Finally I am including a mood board on this main page. A mood board is, from what I understand of it, just a selection of images that I find inspiring, interesting, and inline with the site's look.
