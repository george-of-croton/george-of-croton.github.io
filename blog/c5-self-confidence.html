Self Awareness

Mindfulness is knowing your self. Knowing the way you feel, your mood, your breath, your heart beat etc. Self-awareness is the act of taking this information and deciding how you are going to use it. for example, you become aware that storires about your coworkers cat are annoying you. You realise that you want to roll your eyes and tell them to be quiet, but you decide the right thing to do is say "awwhh hahaha". You mix in a small loss of dignity with a little bit of dishonesty and you've got yourself a happier coworker. That's self awareness I think.

Another idea covered in the video was "my emotions are not me". The basic concept is that we can train our minds to recognise that we are experiencing an emotion in our bodies without allowing the emotion to impair our judgement. Emotions, such as anger, embarassment, lust can really affect the way we think and often in negative ways. These emotions can cause us to act impulsively rather how we know we should. Of course its good to feel emotions but knowing when not to allow yourself to be controlled by your emotions is definitely a useful skill!
